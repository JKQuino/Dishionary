DISHIONARY GUIDE

Find recipes that use as many of the given ingredients.

  1. Type the ingredients. For Example: "chicken"
  2. Then tap the icon to add it in the list that will be displayed. Add as much ingredients you have.
    *Just tap the ingredient if you want to remove it in list
  3. Once you have listed your ingredients tap the button and recipes with the ingredients will be displayed.
